## Why use a monthly budget planner?

Most budgets fail because they are too rigid or too vague. DailyLogic's Budget Planner focuses on **one month at a time** with a clear safety buffer, so you can plan realistically instead of guessing.

## What you can do

- Set income, fixed costs, and flexible spending categories
- Add a monthly safety buffer for unexpected expenses
- Run gap analysis when expenses exceed income
- Use sliders to explore trade-offs before committing

## Who it's for

This tool works well if you want a **simple, visual plan** without linking bank accounts or exporting to Excel. Everything stays in your browser.

<Callout title="Tip">
Start with your fixed costs first — rent, utilities, subscriptions — then allocate what's left to flexible categories.
</Callout>

## Getting started

Open the live Budget Planner, enter your numbers, and adjust until the gap closes. Save your calculation to pick up later from the dashboard.
