Tipping can feel like a social minefield. Did the courier deserve more because it was raining? Should you tip the same for a coffee as you do for a full sit-down dinner?

### The Philosophy of the Tip

At its core, a tip is a token of appreciation for service. But in a fast-paced world, it's easy to lose track of what's fair. The goal shouldn't be to follow arbitrary rules, but to acknowledge service that goes above and beyond—or to ensure fair compensation for standard service.

### Factors that Actually Matter

When deciding on a tip, consider the 'three pillars of service':

1. **The Human Factor:** Was the service friendly and attentive?
2. **The Effort Factor:** Was the delivery on time despite difficult conditions?
3. **The Value Factor:** Does this service save you time or improve your experience?
