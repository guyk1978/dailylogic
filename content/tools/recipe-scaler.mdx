## Scale without the guesswork

Changing serving sizes usually means mental math and messy notes. Recipe Scaler adjusts every ingredient proportionally and groups them by category for easy reading.

## What you get

- Unit dropdowns for common cooking measurements
- Per-ingredient category grouping
- A compiled scaled recipe you can copy in one click
- Everything runs locally — no account needed

## Great for

Meal prep, batch cooking, halving recipes, and adapting cookbooks to your household size.
